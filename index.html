People commonly associate the word bullying with high school students who are immature. People of all ages can be bullied including adults in a work setting or even family members. We hope you leave this interactive knowing that it is easier said than done for someone to walk away from a bullying situation. This interactive will prompt you to make decisions based off of common situations high school bully victims face.

O N L I N E

One day, when you were on the Internet working on your research paper, your friends sent you a picture that was shared on social media. Someone took a picture of you and wrote "Four eyes," "Fish lips," "Ugly,” and "Loser." Once you see the picture, do you want to report it, ignore it, or take it as a joke and laugh?

R E P O R T

The school talks to the people who took the picture and suspends them. After a while they return and continue to bully you again. Do you want to report them again or physically fight back?

I G N O R E

After you decided to ignore the post made about you, you went to school. While you were in the hallway, people constantly started laughing at you. Do you ask them why, tell the administration, or walk away?

R E S P O N D

Because you responded to the post, the administration found out who you were, and you were suspended from school because the administration classified what you said as pure retaliation and not self defense. If you wish to continue and change your ending pick a different route starting from your first decision.

R E P O R T A G A I N

Although this is the second time you reported the bully, the problem didn’t stop. As a result, you believe you were left with no choice but to stay there and tolerate the bullying, or to switch schools. You decided to switch schools, and at your new school, you still got bullied for being an outsider. You made it to graduation. If you wish to continue and change your ending, pick a different route starting from your first decision.

F I G H T B A C K

Because you decided to fight back, the administration didn’t care to hear your side of the story. Instead, they suspended you from school, which later went on your permanent record. If you wish to continue and change your ending, pick a different route starting from your first decision.

A S K

After you ask the people why they were laughing at you, they responded by saying how you’re a punk and they will never be your friend. Although what they said was really hurtful, you were able to look past that and graduated school without any help from them or anyone else that tried to put you down.

T E L L

You told the principal that some kids were laughing at you and the principal said that you were over-reacting and you should get over it.

A witness volunteered to help you explain what happened. In the end, the principal finally took action and got the bully suspended.

W A L K A W A Y

You avoided the problem for now, but as you walk away you can tell that this is not going to stop. The problem didn’t stop immediately but you learned to not let the bullies bother you anymore. After a while they realized they were wrong for laughing at you. If you wish to continue and change your ending, pick a different route starting from your first decision.
